About Eve:

  • Released two self-produced albums
  • Indie album "Bunka" released on December 13, 2017
  • First album with entirely self composed songs
  • Track "Dramaturgy" has over 180 million plays


Albums:

  • "Otogi" released on February 6, 2019 - ranked 2nd on the Oricon album chart
  • "Smile" released on February 12, 2020 - ranked 2nd on the Oricon album chart
  • "Kaikai Kitan and Ao no Waltz" released on December 23, 2020 - ranked 1st on the Oricon daily ranking
  • Track "Kaikai Kitan" streamed 700 million times worldwide
  • Music video played over 360 million times resulting to most played Japanese song overseas on Spotify in 2021
  • "Kaizin" released in March 2022 which ranked 3rd on the Oricon album daily chart


Tours:

  • His largest hall tour, "Eve Live Tour 2022 Kaizin," attracted around 25000 people
  • Sold out additional performance at Nippon Budokan in August 2022
  • First arena tour, "Eve Arena Tour 2023 Kororai," held in August 2023
  • Largest capacity two-day performance, "Eve Live 2023 Karan," at Saitama Super Arena, attracting over 35000 people
  • During his first Asia tour, "Eve Asia Tour 2024 Culture," from May to June 2024, he visited seven cities including Japan which sold out immediately
  • Eve attracts great attention not only in Japan but also overseas
